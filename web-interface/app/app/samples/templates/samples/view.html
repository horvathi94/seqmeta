Samples

Manage samples. To register new samples use the editor, by selecting the desired template and clicking the Register samples button.

To modify existing samples, mark the checkbox next to the samples and click the Edit selectedbutton.

To delete any samples, mark the checkbox next to the samples and click the Delete samples button.

To start a submission process, mark the checkbox next to the samples, which you wish to upload and click the button for the desired submission.
